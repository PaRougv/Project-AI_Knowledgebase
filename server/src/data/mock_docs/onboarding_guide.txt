New Hire Onboarding Guide

Accounts:
- Your email and SSO accounts are provisioned on your start date.
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all systems.

First week checklist:
- Read the company handbook and security policy.
- Attend the product overview session on Wednesday at 2 PM.
- Set up your development environment with the standard toolchain.

Support:
- For access issues, open a ticket with IT.
- For payroll or benefits questions, contact HR.

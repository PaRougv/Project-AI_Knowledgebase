Customer Returns & Refunds Policy

Return window:
- Physical products can be returned within 30 days of delivery.
- Software subscriptions can be canceled for a prorated refund within 14 days.

Eligibility:
- Items must be in original packaging.
- Proof of purchase is required.
- Personalized items are not eligible for return.

Refund timeline:
- Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days after approval.
- Original payment method will be used when possible.

Escalations:
- Chargebacks should be routed to the finance team.
- VIP customers should be prioritized within 24 hours.

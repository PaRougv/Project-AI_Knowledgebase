Company Handbook (2026 Edition)

Work hours: Standard hours are 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM local time, Monday to Friday.
Flexible start times are allowed within a 90-minute window if your manager approves.

Time off:
- Employees accrue 1.5 days of paid leave per month.
- Unused leave can be carried forward up to 10 days into the next year.
- Sick leave is separate from paid leave and does not require advance approval.

IT policy:
- Company laptops must use full-disk encryption.
- Do not share passwords or API keys in chat or email.
- Use the approved password manager for all work accounts.

Support escalation:
Tier 1 handles basic requests. Tier 2 handles account changes and refunds.
Critical incidents require immediate escalation to the on-call manager.
